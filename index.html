Our Journals

Peace of Mind Journals are daily planners and self-care journals all in one. Through understanding that life is hectic and it's easy to feel lost inthe choas.

Our journals are carefully crafted to ease your anxiety. They take tasks and subjects that seem heavy and give you the power to tackle anything that stands in your way.

Keep track of daily tasks, meal habits, and wellness through journaling and log activities. Give yourself a peace of mind and open one of our journals today!

Our Journey

Peace of Mind is created by a busy college student who struggled to manage daily tasks while trying to be successful.

Through working herself to burnout all throughout school, our CEO always looks for ways to balance her wellness better. She loved the idea of journaling, meal prep, and calendaring!

It just felt too overwhelming peace of mind. Peace of Mind Journals was created to make all that easier and more accessible.
